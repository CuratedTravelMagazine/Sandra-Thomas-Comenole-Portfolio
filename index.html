BIO

Sandra Thomas-Comenole is a marketing strategist and behavioral growth expert who thrives at the intersection of creativity, analytics, and empathy. One CEO called her a “miracle worker” for turning companies around, with two decades of helping brands across hospitality, travel, SaaS, luxury lifestyle, and biotech grow with purpose.

She builds human-centered, data-driven strategies that deliver measurable impact: 3x revenue growth, multi-million-dollar turnarounds, 254% organic traffic increases, 153% conversion lifts, and viral campaigns reaching hundreds of thousands. She leads global teams of 20+ and crafts strategies that balance bold ideas with disciplined execution.

Sandra holds an M.S. and B.S. in Economics from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and is based in Troy, NY, usually with an iced in hand, and a sunrise or sunset in a new city reminding her why she loves travel.
